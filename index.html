The Extended Learning Area is one out of three. There is one area for each floor. The purpose of these areas is to do something new in a fun way or to have some privacy. I vote the extended learning areas the best kind of classroom!

What's Up

  • The way Kindness Rocks works is this. You paint a rock and write a kind message. You leave the rock outside your house. Then, after some time, someone will pick it up. Once they read it, you'll make their day.

  • The water project is a fundraiser to help the people who don't have water because of hurricanes. Once every year, the kids who serve in the project will all meet up in the gym. They all start singing about how importent it is to save water. They than tell you where to find them. It's a really nice fundraiser, and it help everyine have clean water to use.
